Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

🔒 Mizan is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect personal data, require account registration, or share your information with third parties.

This Privacy Policy explains how Mizan ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. Mizan is an Islamic companion app providing prayer times, Qibla direction, mosque timetables, and prayer notifications.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

Mizan requests access to your device's location to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times based on your exact coordinates
  • Determine your Qibla (direction to the Kaaba in Makkah)
  • Display your city name in the app header

Your location is never transmitted to our servers, stored, or shared with any third party. It is used exclusively on your device for the above purposes.

Compass / Motion Data

The Qibla screen uses your device's compass sensor to show the direction of the Kaaba. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

Preferences & Settings

Mizan stores your preferences locally on your device using AsyncStorage, including:

  • Selected prayer calculation method (e.g. Muslim World League)
  • Prayer mode (Auto Calculate or Mosque Timetable)
  • Selected mosque
  • Notification settings and reminder preferences per prayer
  • Theme preference (Light, Dark, or Auto)

All preferences are stored on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Notification Data

If you enable prayer notifications, Mizan schedules local notifications on your device. These are processed entirely on your device — no notification content or scheduling data is sent to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Mizan uses the following external services to function:

Aladhan API (aladhan.com)

Used to calculate prayer times. When calculating, your latitude, longitude, and selected calculation method are sent to Aladhan's servers. No personal information is included. See Aladhan's Privacy Policy.

Mizan Mosque Timetable API

When you select a mosque in Mosque Timetable mode, the app contacts our API server to retrieve live prayer and Jamaat times for that mosque. Only the mosque identifier is sent — no personal data, no location coordinates. The API is hosted on secure infrastructure and does not log user requests.

Supported mosques at launch:

  • Glasgow Central Mosque
  • Edinburgh Central Mosque
  • East London Mosque
  • Birmingham Central Mosque

4. How We Use Information

The limited data described above is used solely to:

  • Calculate and display accurate prayer times for your location
  • Show the correct Qibla direction
  • Retrieve live mosque timetable data
  • Schedule prayer notifications on your device
  • Remember your preferences between sessions

5. Data Storage & Security

All app preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not operate databases containing personal user information. Uninstalling Mizan from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

Mizan is suitable for users of all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. No account creation or personal information is required to use the app.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Since Mizan does not collect or process personal data on our servers, traditional data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure) do not apply in the conventional sense. However:

  • Right to erasure: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Right to portability: Your preferences exist only on your device
  • Questions: Contact us at the address below for any privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users through the app. Continued use of Mizan after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
